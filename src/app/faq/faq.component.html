Even if my child completes the chapters and all the quizzes successfully, Argus doesn't reflect it under completed. How can we achieve 100% completion of the chapter?

Every chapter/ week in Argus is timed for a particular duration. For example: Science chapter 1 to be completed between April 1 to April 7. If the learner completes the task on April 3, the chapter will still show completed only on April 8, after the scheduled duration.
